Our Story

Where luxury and nature create unforgettable experiences

Nestled in the scenic landscapes of Machakos County, Sun Glam Hotel Machakos was born from a vision to create a sanctuary where guests can reconnect with nature without compromising on luxury and comfort.

Our resort is strategically located just 1 minute from Machakos Town and Kangundo Road, offering modern rooms, elegant interiors, and breathtaking views of the Eastern Kenya hills.

What makes Sun Glam truly unique is our commitment to authentic local experiences, from our delicious Kienyeji chicken prepared with traditional recipes to our serene environment that captures the essence of Kenyan hospitality.
