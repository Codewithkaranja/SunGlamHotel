Our gourmet dining experience is curated by award-winning chefs who blend international culinary techniques with authentic Kenyan flavors. We source our ingredients locally to ensure freshness and support our community.

From intimate candlelit dinners to grand banquet celebrations, our restaurant offers versatile dining options for every occasion.

  • Farm-to-table fresh ingredients
  • Specialized Kienyeji chicken dishes

Refresh & Rejuvenate

Our Olympic-sized swimming pool is surrounded by lush greenery, creating a serene oasis for relaxation and recreation. The pool area features comfortable loungers, poolside service, and a separate children's pool for family enjoyment.

Whether you're looking to swim laps for exercise or simply float while enjoying the beautiful Machakos scenery, our pool provides the perfect setting.

  • Olympic dimensions (50m x 25m)
  • Temperature-controlled water
  • Dedicated children's pool area
  • Poolside bar and snack service
